Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Brandon Clement ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Yada - Intentional Questions mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

When you use Yada, you may provide:

  • Personal responses: Your answers to daily questions
  • Custom questions: Questions you create
  • Journey entries: Personal reflections and notes
  • Preferences: App settings and theme choices

2. Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect:

  • Usage data: How you interact with the App
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash logs to improve stability

3. Purchase and Subscription Information

When you make in-app purchases or subscribe:

  • All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store
  • We receive only transaction receipts and identifiers
  • We do NOT collect or store payment card details
  • Subscription status is stored to provide premium features
  • Purchase history is maintained for customer support purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Sync your data across your devices (via iCloud)
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Process premium subscriptions
  • Send notifications (if enabled)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App

Data Storage and Security

iCloud Sync

  • Your personal data is stored in your private iCloud container
  • Only you can access your iCloud data
  • We cannot access your iCloud-stored content
  • Data syncs across your devices signed into the same Apple ID

Local Storage

  • Data is stored locally on your device
  • Local data is encrypted using iOS security features

Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No server-side storage of personal content
  • Minimal data collection philosophy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Access your personal journal entries
  • Store your data on our servers

We MAY share data:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or property
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your data within the App
  • Correction: Edit or update your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and notifications

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Most data can be managed directly in the App settings
  • For account deletion: Delete the App and your iCloud data
  • For other requests: Contact us at support@yadaquestions.com

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

The App uses:

  • App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • iCloud: For data synchronization
  • StoreKit: For subscription and purchase management

Subscription Management

  • Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID
  • Auto-renewal settings can be changed in your Apple ID Account Settings
  • Subscription information is synced across your devices
  • We verify subscription status through Apple's servers

Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Analytics

We use minimal, privacy-focused analytics to improve the App:

  • No personal information is collected
  • Data is aggregated and anonymized
  • You can opt-out in Settings

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while you use the App
  • Deleted app: Local data removed immediately
  • iCloud data: Managed according to your iCloud settings
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained for 25 months

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Note: We do not sell personal information.

European Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing your data
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to object to processing
